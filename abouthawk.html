ABOUT HAWK

Introduction: Rainbow Hi-tech engineering company Pvt Ltd was founded in March 2013.It has been one of the leading battery manufacturing companies across Pakistan. After pioneering successful Conventional Batteries i.e Bridge Power and 4G batteries, the Rainbow Hi-Tech Engineering Pvt Ltd has emerged as an exceptional superior company producing conventional and maintenance-free batteries for convenience of Pakistan’s automobile industry and having the widespread recognition in the masses for its superiority and for its quality.
The Rainbow Hi-Tech Engineering Company is certified as QMS-ISO 9001:2015 & EMS-ISO 14001-2015 by SGS.
Now , In addition to this, Rainbow Hi-Tech Engineering Company has proudly Introduced Calcium (+) Sealed Maintenance free batteries.
